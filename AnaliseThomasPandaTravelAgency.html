Did you ever have an interest in California?

In California there is a beach called Pfeiffer Beach in Big Sur: Purple Sand, Sea Caves & Dog Friendly. The beach can be hard to find, but if you head South it is the second road after Pfeiffer State Park. The purple sand is best after it rains. And the beach is dog friendly! The parking lot is small so when you. park you have to wait for people to exit especially during the summer. The parking is five dollars.

When you walk to the beach you are immediately greeted with the amazing views and sheer beauty of Pfeiffer State Beach. Pfeiffer Beach has a good amount of people, it is not crowded like Southern California.

This beach also has amazing purple sand. The washing of the rocks gives the sand a purple color. You might not always get to see the purple sand every time you go to the beach. If you head South,especially after it rains there is a good chance that you will see the view below.
